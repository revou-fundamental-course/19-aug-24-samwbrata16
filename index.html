Our Profile

Moonstone is a fictional independent bookstore/cafe based in Jakarta, Indonesia. Founded in 2014, we provide imported novels and comics/manga. We also provide a cozy place where you can hang out and read while having a cup of coffee, tea and/or snacks.

What We Offer

Import Novels and Comics/Manga of Many Genres
Reasonable Prices
Cozy Spot to Read and Hangout
A Variety of Drinks and Snacks
